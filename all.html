About RiseIntern

RiseIntern bridges the gap between students and meaningful internship opportunities. RiseIntern is a smart internship discovery platform designed to connect students with the right opportunities at the right time. Our mission is to bridge the gap between talented youth and meaningful career pathways by offering AI-powered recommendations, personalized dashboards, and direct integration with government schemes like the PM Internship Program. With a simple, student-friendly interface, RiseIntern empowers learners to explore diverse domains such as software development, data science, digital marketing, finance, and more. 🌟
